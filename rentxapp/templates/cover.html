About Us

We are group of fresh developers who are passionate about the environment. In our start-up we are trying to reduce products’ consumption and the burden of products’ ownership. For that, we created a website that encourage sharing our products with others. Rent_X is a website that will allow university students living in accommodations to share/rent their own products. In this website students can register to offer their products for free or in exchange for small amount of money.
